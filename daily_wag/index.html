About Us


At The Daily Wag, we believe every dog deserves a happy tail and a healthy walk.
Our mission is to provide loving, reliable, and professional care to every furry friend, ensuring their happiness and your peace of mind.


We know that every dog is more than just a pet—they're family. That's why we're committed to providing tailored care, fun-filled walks, and the attention your furry companion deserves. With us, every leash leads to trust, safety and wagging tails.
